About Our SpecialTea

Who We Are

Our SpecialTea is a multi-brand tea subscription box service and online tea shop founded in 2020, at the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic. Our warehouse is based in Brooklyn, NY, but we offer our services and ship nationwide. The mission of Our SpecialTea is to provide affordable, top-quality loose-leaf, packaged, and bottled teas in personalized subscription boxes, delivered directly to your door, as well as offer all of our teas in an online shop for you to purchase directly, without having to commit to a subscription. Our teas, whether ordered in a subscription box or from our online shop, are delivered straight to your door.


What Makes Us Special

We are different from other online tea service companies, as we have a shop separate from our subscription service, offer iced, sparkling, and bottled tea selections in addition to hot tea blends, and we provide multiple tea brands rather than just our own. We created Our SpecialTea with the pandemic in mind: if a you don't want to go out due to the pandemic or simply don't want to deal with the weather, you can still get your favorite teas straight to your door.


Why You Should Choose Us

Every Saturday, we donate 5% of our profits to a charity that we've selected for their immeasurable impact on our community. We've donated over $20,000 to 24 nonprofits since the initiative began.
